# Privacy Policy — VaultCam

*Effective date: June 21, 2026*

## Overview

VaultCam is a privacy-first encrypted camera app. All photos and videos you capture are encrypted and stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data or media.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information directly.**

VaultCam does not collect, store, or transmit:

- Photos, videos, or any media you capture or import
- Your name, email address, or any account information
- Location data
- Usage analytics or crash reports

However, the app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information to serve ads (see "Advertising" section below).

## How Your Data Is Stored

All media files are encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption. Vault access is protected by a password you choose. Your password is never stored in plaintext — it is used to derive an encryption key via Argon2id key derivation. Neither we nor any third party can decrypt your data.

## Advertising

VaultCam uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Ads are shown only once per session on a dedicated interstitial screen after you agree to this Privacy Policy, and never inside your encrypted vault.

Google AdMob may collect and use the following information to serve relevant ads:

- Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
- IP address and general location (country/region level)
- Device type, operating system version, and app information
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy. You can review Google's privacy practices at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**Opting out of personalized ads:** You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time in your device settings:
- Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

## Permissions

VaultCam requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Camera | Capture photos and videos |
| Microphone | Record audio for videos |
| Storage / Media access | Import media from your device gallery |
| Internet | Load and display advertisements |

## Third-Party Services

VaultCam integrates Google AdMob for advertising purposes only. No analytics, crash reporting, or other tracking SDKs are used. Your media files and vault data are never shared with any third party.

| Third Party | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Google AdMob | In-app advertising | [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

## Children's Privacy

VaultCam is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the new version here with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
