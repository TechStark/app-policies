# Privacy Policy — VaultCam

*Effective date: June 21, 2026*

## Overview

VaultCam is a privacy-first encrypted camera app. All photos and videos you capture are encrypted and stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data or media.

## Information We Collect

**We collect no personal information.**

VaultCam does not collect, store, or transmit:

- Photos, videos, or any media you capture or import
- Your name, email address, or any account information
- Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or hardware information
- Location data
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Any other personal or sensitive data

## How Your Data Is Stored

All media files are encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption. Vault access is protected by a password you choose. Your password is never stored in plaintext — it is used to derive an encryption key via Argon2id key derivation. Neither we nor any third party can decrypt your data.

## Permissions

VaultCam requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Camera | Capture photos and videos |
| Microphone | Record audio for videos |
| Storage / Media access | Import media from your device gallery |

These permissions are used only within the app and are never used to collect or transmit data.

## Third-Party Services

VaultCam does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with any third party.

## Children's Privacy

VaultCam does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the new version here with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
